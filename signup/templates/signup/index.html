About Us
Vision

ThErudite was brought about in March 2017 on the belief that the girls, if given more opportunities and financial aids to pursue higher education, can bring about a positive difference to the society and the world at large.

Endeavors for Students

ThErudite helps the girls find best suited scholarships according to their profile. ThErudite, on its end, tries to narrow down the choices and hence, reducing the time wasted to scan through all the scholarships by the candidate.

Endeavors for Organisations

ThErudite gives a platform to the universities to select best candidates and gives access of the documents of their documents when an application is received.
